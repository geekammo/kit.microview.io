Circuit 6 - Temperature Sensor
Insert your MicroView and Programmer with Pin 1 in hole C7.
Insert your Temperature Sensor so it’s in points A14, A15 and A16.
Ground Pin
Insert your Blue Jumper between points B13 and B15.
Insert your Red Jumper between points I8 and E16.
Connect your MicroView to your computer’s USB Port.
Click on the “Run on Arduino” to put this code on your MicroView.
