Circuit 10 - Relay
Insert your MicroView and Programmer with Pin 1 in hole C4.
Incase you missed it, unlike the previous tutorials, we’ve moved the Programmer left to hole C4.
Insert a 330Ω Resistor between points G12 and D12.
Insert a Diode between points F13 and D13.
Insert a Transistor in points B11, B12 and B13.
Insert a Red Jumper between points I5 and Y7.
Insert a Yellow Jumper between points J9 and J12.
Insert a Black Jumper between points A11 and X12.
Insert a Blue Jumper between points J13 and J15.
Insert a Yellow Jumper between points C13 and C15.
Insert a Red Jumper between points I15 and Y17.
Insert a your Relay with it’s pins in points F15, E15, F19, E20 and F21.
Insert another 330Ω Resistor between points C20 and W22.
Insert a Yellow LED between points C23 and C24.
Insert a Red LED between points C26 and C27.
Insert a Yellow Jumper between points I21 and E23.
Insert a Purple Jumper between points J19 and E26.
Insert a Yellow Jumper between points A24 and X28.
Insert a Yellow Jumper between points A27 and X29.
Insert a Red Jumper between points Y31 and W31.
Connect your MicroView to your computer’s USB Port.
Click on the “Run on Arduino” to put this code on your MicroView.
