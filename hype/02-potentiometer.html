Circuit 2 - Potentiometer
Insert your MicroView and Programmer with Pin 1 in hole C7.
Insert your Potentiometer into points E18, E19 and E20 of your breadboard.
Insert your Black Jumper between points B14 and B18.
Insert your Blue Jumper between points A12 and A19.
Insert your Red Jumper between points J8 and B20.
Connect your MicroView to your computer’s USB Port.
Click on the “Run on Arduino” to put this code on your MicroView.
