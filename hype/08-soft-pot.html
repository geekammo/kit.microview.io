Circuit 11 - Soft Potentiometer
Insert your MicroView and Programmer with Pin 1 in hole C7.
Insert your Red Jumper into points I8 and W4.
Insert a 330 Ohm resistor between points B13 and B23.
Insert another 330 Ohm Resistor between points A22 and W23.
Insert a Black Jumper into points A14 and A24.
Insert your Soft Potentiometer into points C22, C23 and C24.
Connect your MicroView to your computer’s USB Port.
Click on the “Run on Arduino” to put this code on your MicroView.
